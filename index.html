Apple Inc. History

Apple Inc. is a technology company founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne.

Tweets by AppleSupport

Key Milestones:

  • 1976: Founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne.
  • 1984: Introduction of the Macintosh, the first successful mass-market personal computer with a graphical user interface.
  • 2001: Launch of the iPod, revolutionizing the music industry and paving the way for iTunes.
  • 2007: Introduction of the iPhone, reshaping the smartphone market and mobile computing.
  • 2010: Release of the iPad, popularizing the tablet computer.
